ABOUT

Alistair Robertson

Based in London, UK, Alistair gained his Undergraduate Degree in Music from the University of Birmingham, before receiving his Masters Degree in Composition for Screen from the Royal College of Music.

He has worked as composer and/or orchestrator on a wide variety of projects – from full orchestras, to rock bands, to Musical Theatre pit ensembles – demonstrating clear command of a range of idioms. His style is influenced heavily by his formal classical training, yet is balanced with a rich, modern cinematic sound.

He has worked with award winning composers on major film, television, and video game projects. He has recorded music at Abbey Road, AIR Studios, the Eastwood Scoring Stage (Warner Brothers), and the Synchron Stage in Vienna.

He has had pieces performed by the Hollywood Studio Symphony Orchestra, the Moravian Philharmonic Orchestra, and the Royal College of Music Film Orchestra, among others.
